About MindTalk

MindTalk is our monthly special event designed to help people build good behavioral and interpersonal values alongside their technical skills and successfully excel anywhere in the world.

Our Vision

To be a guiding force, fostering holistic development, and creating a community where students emerge as resilient, socially conscious, and enterprising individuals.
